This program works with particular set of colors:
    W - White
    G - Green
    R - Red
    B - Blue
    O - Orange
    Y - Yellow

Format is pretty staightforward. Only first 54 non-space 
characters are considered, so here you can write comments.
Red side should be in front, white on top.
